Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 14, 2026 GDPR & CCPA Compliant

Active Energy Boost (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, book consultations, or engage with our elite fitness and wellness services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that identifies, relates to, describes, or is capable of being associated with you (“Personal Information”). Categories include:

  • Identity Data: Full name, date of birth, gender, emergency contact details.
  • Contact Data: Email address, phone number, billing address, postal code.
  • Health & Fitness Data: Physical activity metrics, injury history, fitness goals, dietary preferences (only with explicit consent).
  • Transaction Data: Membership payments, class bookings, product purchases.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, cookies, and usage analytics.
  • Marketing Preferences: Your subscription status and communication choices.

We may also collect anonymous demographic information that is not unique to you.

2. How We Collect Your Information

We use different methods to collect data from and about you:

  • Direct interactions: When you fill out consultation forms, register for events, contact our concierge, or purchase memberships.
  • Automated technologies: As you navigate our site, cookies and similar tracking technologies log your interaction patterns. See our Cookies Policy for details.
  • Third parties: Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics), payment processors, and social media platforms (if you interact with our branded content).

3. Use of Your Personal Data

We only process your personal information where we have a legal basis to do so. Common purposes include:

  • ✓ To deliver personalized fitness plans and nutritional guidance.
  • ✓ To manage memberships, bookings, and payments for premium services.
  • ✓ To communicate essential service updates, respond to inquiries, or schedule consultations.
  • ✓ To improve our website, luxury facilities, and client experience through analytics.
  • ✓ To send marketing communications (with your consent) about exclusive events, offers, or wellness insights.
  • ✓ To comply with legal obligations and protect the security of our community.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties for monetary compensation. Limited data sharing may occur with trusted partners (e.g., payment gateways, IT services) bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For individuals in the European Economic Area (EEA), our processing relies on:

  • Performance of a contract: To provide our fitness services and manage your membership.
  • Legitimate interests: To enhance services, prevent fraud, and maintain security.
  • Consent: For direct marketing, health data collection, and certain analytics cookies.
  • Legal obligation: When required by law or regulatory authorities.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

Active Energy Boost may share personal information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal), email delivery platforms, CRM software, and IT support — all subject to data protection agreements.
  • Professional Advisors: Lawyers, auditors, and insurers where necessary to protect our rights.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred with notice.
  • Legal Requirements: To comply with subpoenas, court orders, or governmental requests.

We never share sensitive health data with advertisers or third-party marketing platforms without explicit opt-in consent.

6. International Data Transfers

As a global wellness brand with locations in the US, UK, and UAE, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. Where required by law, we implement Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent safeguards to ensure adequate protection. By using our services, you acknowledge that your data may be stored on secure servers located in the United States and the European Union.

7. Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-leading technical and organizational measures: encrypted databases, SSL/TLS transmission, role-based access controls, and continuous monitoring. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, plus any legal retention period (e.g., tax records, health & safety logs). Typically:

  • Contact & membership data: Duration of active membership + 3 years after cancellation.
  • Health & fitness data: 2 years after last interaction, unless you request deletion earlier.
  • Marketing data: Until you unsubscribe or withdraw consent.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., California, EU, UK), you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Obtain a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure (“Right to be Forgotten”): Request deletion of your data under certain conditions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data while we verify compliance.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Opt-out of direct marketing or processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: At any time, for processing based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@activeenergyboost.com or call +1 (800) 555-3647. We will respond within 30 days. No fee will be charged unless requests are manifestly unfounded or excessive.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are designed for individuals aged 16 and older (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a minor has provided us with data, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to request disclosure of categories and specific pieces of personal information collected, request deletion, and opt-out of any “sale” of personal information (we do not sell). To submit a verifiable consumer request, email privacy@activeenergyboost.com or call our toll-free line. Authorized agents may act on your behalf with proper written permission.

11. Direct Marketing & Opt-Out

We respect your inbox. You may unsubscribe from promotional emails by clicking the “unsubscribe” link in any marketing email or adjusting preferences in your account portal. Even after opting out, we may send transactional messages (e.g., booking confirmations, policy updates). SMS marketing requires separate opt-in consent; reply STOP to cancel.

12. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use essential, functional, and analytics cookies to improve your experience. For detailed information on how we use cookies, your choices to manage them, and third-party tracking, please refer to our dedicated Cookies Policy. You may also adjust your browser settings to reject cookies; however, some site features may not function properly.

13. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to partner wellness brands, social media platforms, or external content. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to read their policies before providing any personal information.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Active Energy Boost may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in legal obligations or operational practices. The “Last Updated” date at the top of this page indicates when revisions were made. In case of material changes, we will provide prominent notice via email or website banner. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

15. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or data subject requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data handling practices, please reach out:

  • Email: privacy@activeenergyboost.com
  • Data Protection Officer: legal@activeenergyboost.com
  • Mailing Address: Active Energy Boost – Privacy Compliance, 100 Wellness Avenue, Suite 400, Beverly Hills, CA 90210, USA
  • Phone (Privacy Inquiries): +1 (310) 555-0192

For EU residents, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

By engaging with Active Energy Boost, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. Your trust is the foundation of our excellence.
