The Origin: Beyond Conventional Fitness

Active Energy Boost was born from a stark realization: the fitness industry was broken. Gyms prioritized volume over value, quick fixes over lasting transformation, and aesthetics over authentic wellness. Our co-founders — elite strength coach Marcus Vega and sports nutrition scientist Dr. Sana Kapoor — envisioned a sanctuary where metabolic science, regenerative protocols, and human psychology converge. In 2015, we opened our first flagship in Los Angeles with a radical promise: treat each member as an unique biological system, not a number.

What started as a boutique training lab for professional athletes and executives soon evolved into a global movement. We pioneered AI-driven form analysis, cryotherapy integration, and neuro-somatic coaching when these terms were unknown in mainstream fitness. Our 4-pillar method — Strength Architecture, Cardio-Metabolic Precision, Nutritional Bio-Individuality, and Recovery Science — remains the gold standard for sustainable high performance. Today, Active Energy Boost studios are architectural marvels that blend biophilic design with cutting-edge equipment, fostering an environment where discipline meets peace.

We are unapologetically premium, but our heart is inclusive. Whether you're a postpartum mother rebuilding core integrity, a CEO managing cortisol, or a collegiate athlete chasing podiums, our team customizes every variable. Our digital platform democratizes elite coaching, offering real-time feedback and community-driven challenges. This is not a transactional membership; it's a partnership in evolution.

The Active Energy Boost Methodology

🏛️ Strength Architecture

Periodized, joint-centric programming that builds resilient muscle and explosive power. We emphasize structural balance, mobility foundations, and progressive overload with data-driven fatigue management.

❤️ Cardio-Metabolic Precision

Zone training, VO2 max optimization, and lactate threshold sessions. From incline treadmill intervals to assault bike sprints — we elevate cardiac efficiency and fat oxidation.

🥑 Nutritional Bio-Individuality

No one-size-fits-all diets. DNA, gut microbiome, and lifestyle analysis drive custom macro plans, meal timing, and supplementation. Food is fuel and medicine.

🌀 Recovery Science

Cryotherapy, Normatec compression, sauna, sleep coaching, and parasympathetic activation. We treat recovery as the hidden half of performance.
