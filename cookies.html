Cookies Policy

Effective: June 14, 2026 Transparency & Control

At Active Energy Boost, we believe in complete transparency about how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies. This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can exercise control over your preferences — because your digital wellness matters just as much as your physical health.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, enhance user experience, and provide anonymized analytics. Cookies may be “session cookies” (deleted when you close your browser) or “persistent cookies” (remain until they expire or you delete them). We also use similar technologies such as web beacons, pixels, and local storage.

2. How We Use Cookies

Active Energy Boost uses cookies for several purposes, categorized below. We never use cookies to collect sensitive health or financial data.

Cookie CategoryPurposeExamples & Duration
Strictly Necessary
(Always active)		 Essential for website functionality and security. They enable core features like secure login, session management, and load balancing. You cannot opt out of these cookies. Session cookies (expire on browser close), CSRF tokens, authentication cookies (persist up to 24h).
Performance & Analytics
(Consent based)		 Collect aggregated data about how visitors interact with our site — which pages are popular, how users navigate, and error rates. This helps us optimize the elite wellness experience. Google Analytics (_ga: 2 years, _gid: 24 hours), Hotjar (session based). Anonymized IP addresses.
Functional & Preference
(Consent based)		 Remember choices you make (language, region, cookie consent status) to provide enhanced, personalized features. Without these, some site elements may be less convenient. cookie_preferences (1 year), language settings (6 months), preferred class booking view.
Marketing & Targeting
(Consent based)		 Limit frequency of ads, measure campaign effectiveness, and deliver relevant promotional content about Active Energy Boost’s events & offerings. We do NOT allow third-party behavioral advertising unless you explicitly approve. LinkedIn Insight, Facebook Pixel (conversion tracking, 90 days max). No cross-site tracking for retargeting without consent.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services integrated into our website, such as analytics providers, video hosting (Vimeo), and social media plugins. These third parties have their own privacy policies. We select partners who prioritize data minimization and GDPR/CCPA compliance. Current third-party cookies may include:

  • Google Analytics: Provides anonymized traffic analysis. Google’s privacy policy applies.
  • Stripe & PayPal: Payment processing cookies (strictly necessary for transactions).
  • Calendly: Booking widget for consultations (functional).
  • YouTube/Vimeo: Embedded fitness trailers (may set cookies if you play videos).

We do not allow uncontrolled third-party ad networks or data brokers to set cookies on our site.

4. Your Control: Managing Cookie Preferences

We respect your autonomy. You can manage or withdraw your consent for non-essential cookies at any time. Options include:

  • Cookie Consent Tool: Click the "Cookie Settings" button below to adjust your preferences for Analytics, Functional, and Marketing categories at any moment. Your choice will be saved for 12 months.
  • Browser Controls: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies via settings (e.g., Chrome > Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies). However, disabling strictly necessary cookies may impair site functionality.
  • Global Privacy Control (GPC): If your browser sends the GPC signal, we will automatically treat it as an opt-out of non-essential cookies, respecting your global preference.

To revoke consent for Google Analytics specifically, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

🍪 Customize your cookie settings

You have the freedom to enable or disable optional cookies. Essential cookies remain active to ensure security and core functionality.

Note: Strictly necessary cookies cannot be disabled. Your preferences will be stored using a persistent cookie (duration: 12 months).

5. Cookie Duration & Retention

Our cookies have varying lifespans: session cookies expire when you close your browser, while persistent cookies last up to 2 years maximum (Google Analytics _ga). We review our cookie inventory every 6 months and delete obsolete cookies. No personal data is stored in cookies beyond what is described — we never store credit card numbers or passwords in cookies.

6. Changes to This Cookies Policy

We may update this policy to reflect evolving technologies, legal standards, or operational needs. The “Last Updated” date at the top indicates the latest revision. If we make significant changes, we will notify you via website banner or email (if you are a registered member). Your continued use after changes implies acceptance.
